First Aid Guides

Essential first aid information for common emergencies

Basic First Aid Steps

For Burns

  1. 1 Cool the burn under cold running water
  2. 2 Remove any jewelry near the burn
  3. 3 Cover with sterile dressing
  4. 4 Seek medical attention if severe

For Cuts

  1. 1 Clean the wound with soap and water
  2. 2 Apply direct pressure to stop bleeding
  3. 3 Apply antibiotic ointment
  4. 4 Cover with sterile bandage
